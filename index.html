Game Selling Project

Ddate : 18/09/3023
A Nigerian client named Chukuwuku Ola, who specializes in Android game development, approached me to sell his game online. I offered him a basic plan for ₹1500, which included the promise to provide him with 5-7 potential clients interested in purchasing his game. Since this was my first project, I worked hard, and as a result, he ended up getting 12 customers. Out of those, one customer purchased the game for ₹3.5 lakhs, even though I knew the game was priced at ₹5,000. Furniture Selling

I had hoped to give this client at least 20 customers, but I couldn’t manage that. Later, the client faced some personal issues and stopped contacting me. CCTV installation

On October 10, 2023, I acquired a client from Hyderabad named Guli Boy CCTV Installation. I initially offered a basic plan, but they declined. Three days later, they called back, stating they could only pay ₹1,500 in three installments.
After discussing with my team, we proposed two installments: ₹1,000 first, then ₹500. They were concerned about not getting customers after payment, so we agreed to start work. Once they gained customers, they expressed satisfaction and offered another ₹500. However, I reminded them about the outstanding ₹500 from the previous installment. They wanted me to start the service first, but I insisted on receiving the full payment first, after which they stopped contacting me.
Later, I discovered that a team member had offered them a lower rate, leading us to stop accepting installment payments.

5 Ways to Enhance Your Online Presence

1. Optimize Your Website for SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is essential for increasing your website's visibility on search engines like Google. Here are some tips to optimize your site:
Keyword Research: Identify relevant keywords that potential customers are searching for and incorporate them into your content.
Quality Content: Create valuable, informative content that answers your audience’s questions.
Mobile Optimization: Ensure your website is mobile-friendly, as a large percentage of users browse on their phones.

2. Leverage Social Media

Social media platforms are powerful tools for building brand awareness and engaging with your audience. To enhance your online presence:
Choose the Right Platforms: Focus on platforms where your target audience is most active, such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or Twitter.
Regular Posting: Share regular updates, promotions, and engaging content to keep your audience informed and interested.
Engage with Followers: Respond to comments, messages, and reviews to foster community and build trust.

3. Create Quality Content

Content is king in the digital space. High-quality content not only attracts visitors but also establishes you as an authority in your industry. Consider:
Blogging: Start a blog to share insights, tips, and industry news that resonates with your audience.
Videos and Infographics: Use engaging formats like videos and infographics to convey information visually.
Guest Blogging: Write guest posts for reputable websites in your industry to reach a broader audience.

4. Engage in Online Communities

Joining and participating in online communities related to your industry can significantly enhance your presence. Here’s how to do it effectively:

Network with Peers: Connect with other professionals, collaborate on projects, or share each other's content to expand your reach.
